Dr. Paul Jebaraj, M.A., M.Phil., Ph.D., PBDCA

The American College is one of the oldest higher educational institutions in India, founded in 1881 by American Christian missionaries. The college began with only four students under the visionary first principal, Rev. G. T. Washburn. Since then, the institution has grown phenomenally in academic excellence and student enrollment.

A pioneer in higher education, the college has continued its rich legacy by training students to serve God and society. It remains dedicated to offering globally relevant courses that equip students with in-demand employability skills.
